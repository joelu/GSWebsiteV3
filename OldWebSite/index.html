OUR  STORY

  • DESIGN & BUILD

    GREENSPACE provides refreshing alternatives to the business-as-usual methods of creating exhibits and environments. We are on a mission to re-evaluate and replace traditional materials, techniques and operational procedures with eco-friendly methods. Our goal is to achieve complete sustainability – send nothing to the landfill. Our clients benefit from our team's expertise because we:

    • Impress with cutting edge design.
    • Craft high-end properties using a multitude of fabrication techniques.
    • Exceed expectations with our service.

  • OUR METHODS

    At GREENSPACE, we help establish a framework for sustainability from start
    to finish:

    • We examine your entire exhibit program, working backwards from your exhibit on the show floor to design.
    • Engineer to reduce show site drayage and shipping costs.
    • Integrate emerging techniques in sustainable manufacturing new
      green materials.
    • Partner with local suppliers to reduce carbon emissions from
      shipping components.
    • Re-use and re-purpose existing components whenever possible.
    • Create and maintain high-end rental properties fabricated from
      sustainable materials.

  • OUR PEOPLE

    Our skilled, dedicated team of designers, craftsmen, and project managers have:

     Engineered and fabricated some of the largest and most complex exhibits
    ever built.

    • Partnered with top designers to bring their vision to fruition.
    • Worked with companies ranging from start-ups to established
      corporate leaders.
    • Travelled across the U.S. and internationally to supervise show installation
      and dismantle.
    • Earned a myriad of design awards.

    OUR PARTNERS

    Our clients and partners are progressive companies who want to make a statement without leaving a footprint.

  • FULL EXHIBITION SERVICE CAPABILITIES

    GREENSPACE provides the services you need to bring your custom exhibit or permanent installation, such as a corporate lobby or visitor center, from concept to completion – using sustainable methods and materials. In addition to 3-D exhibit design and fabrication, we are a single source solution for complete exhibition management.

    DESIGN & FABRICATION

    • Conceptual design and renderings.
    • Value and structural engineering.
    • Full graphic services – design and production.
    • High quality fabrication.

    PROJECT & SERVICES MANAGEMENT

    • On-site supervision and coordination of services.
    • Logistics management (Shipping, Installation & Dismantle).
    • Exhibitor show forms management.
    • Audio visual, computer and specialty lighting programs.
    • Storage, maintenance and warehousing.
    • Inventory and asset management.

  • OUR HISTORY

    GREENSPACE was launched in Hillsboro, Oregon, in 2008 from a small team of trade-show exhibit design and fabrication veterans who combined their industry expertise with their environmental commitment to ‘‘rethink everything’’ wasteful in tradeshow practices. Today, the team has grown to 46 employees, who continue to search for sustainable solutions that reduce costs.

  • WHAT WE'RE DOING

    Energy efficiency

    • Solar panels offset more than 90% of company energy usage.
    • Our website is hosted with 100% solar energy.
    • All facility lighting is fluorescent. Occupancy sensors are set for lights in common rooms.

    Green Operations

    • We stick to a comprehensive Recycling Program (which includes wood, cardboard/paper, metal, plastic, and glass).
    • We use 100% recycled copier paper, reusuable dishware and
      green-cleaning products.

    Sustainable Construction

    • Locally-sourced FSC certified plywood and sustainable woods are selected whenever possible.
    • Our adhesive and finishing materials are low VOC and non-toxic.
    • Reclaimed and refurbished items are used to reduce consumption of
      new materials.
    • For retired exhibits, we properly dismantle, recycle, and dispose
      all components.
    • Our crate construction reduces the weight of each crate by 70 pounds for lighter shipping.

    Environmental Stewardship

    • We’re a member of the Conservation Alliance, a collection of organizations that protect threatened wild habitat and outdoor recreation places across America.
